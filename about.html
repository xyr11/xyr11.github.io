About XYR.codes

This is XYR.codes, the official webpage of Xyrus Kurt Roldan in the depths of the internet! Here, you can find all my projects and tutorials that I make, along with some of my best photos taken and sometimes blogs about stuff!

About the creator

Xyrus Kurt Roldan is a student and a self-confessed programming addict, but he is still new in the programming world. He enjoys programming and web developing in his free time, along with taking pictures and playing video games! His interest started when he was in 6th grade when he was first introduced in HTML and CSS, and has grown interest to programming ever since. He has a basic knowledge and understanding of C++, Javascript and Nodejs, and has a strong knowledge at front-end web development with HTML and CSS.
