About XYR.site

XYR.site is a Computer Science 3 project that is being actively developed by Xyrus Roldan for completion of the said course and (more recently) has evolved to encompass for his personal use.

About the owner

Xyrus Kurt Roldan is a student and a self-confessed programming addict, but he is still starting in the programming world. He enjoys programming and web developing in his free time. His interest started when he was in 6th grade when he was first introduced in HTML and CSS, and has grown interest to programming ever since. He has a basic knowledge and understanding of C++, Javascript and Nodejs, and is skilled at front-end web development with HTML and CSS. See his projects here.
