“Concentration”

October 04, 2020

A Shakespearean sonnet-inspired poem about concentration

Concentration

A painful thought as he foretold
Still lingering last yesterday
I’m getting on and getting gold
I’ll just do work, no time to play

Regrettably, you need to go
You should have your own self-control
A fact you should know: “time’s your foe”
Get good grades for graduation roll

For every second of our life
The value of them is precious gold
(You) concentrate and use your time
So tomorrow you’ll be strong and bold

Like everyone on planet earth
(You) concentrate; now and henceforth

First seen at PSHS-CVC KHub - English 3 course
