Journal: The Rich and the Poor

October 04, 2020

The gap between the rich and the poor is still getting larger despite all the years of government efforts.

Since the founding of the Republic of the Philippines, this problem is addressed in many ways by presidents and leaders, but none of them are close at achieving the ultimate goal: equality on all Filipinos by financial means. Support from the government is much more needed by the poor especially in these times.

But it seems that support from the government even dwindled, and if the proper actions aren't made, the country would be in a tough situation.
